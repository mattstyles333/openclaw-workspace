## ❌ TOP 5 CUSTOMER PAIN POINTS

### 1. **Stock Availability Issues** (Most Common Complaint)
- Frames showing as available but actually out of stock
- Designer frames ordered from Italy causing delays (1-3 weeks)
- Customer: "Glasses on website BUT NOT in stock"
- Disappointment when frames discontinued after ordering

### 2. **Delivery Delays & Communication Gaps**
- Orders taking 3-6 weeks instead of promised timeframe
- Lack of proactive updates on delays
- Quality control failures causing additional delays
- Confusion during holiday periods

### 3. **Hidden/Unexpected Costs**
- Prescription surcharges applied after ordering (£30+ for prism/complex prescriptions)
- Home trial £0.01 offer with £39.99 charge if not returned in 14 days (terms buried)
- Confusion over reglaze pricing (base price + lens costs)
- Customer: "Dozen emails for a single order... so many upsells"

### 4. **Quality Control Issues**
- Lenses loose in frame upon arrival
- Prescription errors (wrong RX, incorrect bifocal positioning)
- Frame damage during transit (poor packaging)
- Anti-fog coatings not performing as expected
- Mixed up orders (swapped lenses between pairs)

### 5. **Communication & Process Frustrations**
- Excessive marketing emails/spam ("7 spam emails over Black Friday")
- Return label system failures
- Phone support difficulties (long waits, unclear explanations)
- Cancellation difficulties (emails ignored, items still dispatched)
