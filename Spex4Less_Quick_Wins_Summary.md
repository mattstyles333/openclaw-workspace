# Spex4Less Conversion Optimization - Quick Wins Summary

## Top 5 High-ROI Tactics (Limited Resources)

### 1. Abandoned Cart Email Sequence (High Impact / Low Effort)
- **What**: 3 automated emails (1h, 24h, 72h after abandonment)
- **Why**: 45% of recoveries happen within 2 hours
- **Expected**: 10-15% recovery rate → potentially £40k+ monthly
- **Effort**: 2 weeks setup
- **Tools**: Existing email platform (Klaviyo, Dotdigital, Mailchimp)

### 2. Referral Program Boost (Medium Impact / Low Effort)
- **What**: Add referral CTAs to transactional emails + account dashboard
- **Why**: Current program exists but under-promoted
- **Expected**: 20% increase in referrals
- **Effort**: 1 week (email template edits)
- **Cost**: £0 (just design time)

### 3. Lens Upgrade Upsells (High Impact / Medium Effort)
- **What**: Offer thinner lenses/coatings during checkout
- **Why**: Eyewear naturally lends to prescription upgrades
- **Expected**: 15-25% take rate, £15-30 AOV increase
- **Effort**: 4 weeks (developer + design)
- **ROI**: High (minimal discounting needed)

### 4. Exit-Intent Popup (Medium Impact / Low Effort)
- **What**: Capture email before visitors leave
- **Offer**: 10% discount for email signup
- **Expected**: 5-15% email capture rate
- **Effort**: 1 week (Shopify app)
- **Cost**: £20-50/month app fee

### 5. Welcome Email Series (High Impact / Medium Effort)
- **What**: 4-email sequence for new subscribers
- **Why**: Convert subscribers to first purchase
- **Expected**: 3-5x ROI on acquisition cost
- **Effort**: 2 weeks setup
- **Tools**: Email automation platform
