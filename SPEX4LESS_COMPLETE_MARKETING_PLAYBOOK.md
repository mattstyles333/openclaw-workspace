## 1. EMAIL MARKETING STRATEGY

### 1.1 Current State Assessment

**Typical Eyewear Retail Email Challenges:**
- Low open rates (industry avg: 15-20% for retail)
- Seasonal purchase cycles (glasses every 1-3 years)
- Prescription complexity creates friction
- High consideration purchase requiring trust

**Spex4Less-Specific Considerations:**
- Online-only model requires higher trust-building
- Price advantage should be highlighted
- Virtual try-on/prescription upload process needs education

### 1.2 Segmentation Strategy

#### Primary Segments

| Segment | Criteria | Key Messaging |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| **New Prospects** | Signed up, no purchase | Education, trust-building, first-purchase discount |
| **First-Time Buyers** | 1 purchase | Thank you, care tips, accessory upsell |
| **Repeat Customers** | 2+ purchases | Loyalty rewards, early access, referral program |
| **Lapsed Customers** | No purchase 18+ months | Win-back offer, "time for a new prescription" |
| **High-Value** | AOV >£150 | VIP treatment, exclusive frames, personal stylist |
| **Prescription Expiring** | Uploaded 22+ months ago | Urgency, re-exam reminder, discount |

#### Behavioral Segments

| Segment | Trigger | Action |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **Cart Abandoners** | Added to cart, no purchase | 3-email sequence (1hr, 24hr, 72hr) |
| **Browse Abandoners** | Viewed 3+ products, no cart | Personalized recommendations |
| **Engaged Non-Buyers** | Opened 5+ emails, no purchase | Special "what's holding you back?" survey |
| **Win-Back Candidates** | Previous customer, 12mo silence | "We miss you" + significant discount |

### 1.3 Automation Workflows

#### Workflow 1: Welcome Series (New Subscribers)

**Trigger:** Email signup (popup, footer, checkout)
**Duration:** 7 days
**Goal:** First purchase

| Day | Email | Content | CTA |
|-----|-------|---------|-----|
| 0 | Welcome | Brand story, what makes Spex4Less different | Browse frames |
| 1 | How It Works | Prescription upload, virtual try-on, delivery | Upload prescription |
| 3 | Social Proof | Reviews, testimonials, before/after | See styles |
| 5 | First Purchase Offer | 10% off first order (time-limited) | Shop now |
| 7 | Last Chance | Final reminder of offer expiration | Claim discount |

#### Workflow 2: Post-Purchase Nurture

**Trigger:** Order confirmation
**Duration:** 90 days
**Goal:** Satisfaction, reviews, referrals

| Timing | Email | Content |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Immediate | Order Confirmed | Details, tracking, care tips |
| Day 3 | How to Adjust | Video guide, fitting tips |
| Day 7 | Review Request | Incentivized review (entry into draw) |
| Day 14 | Accessory Upsell | Cases, cleaning kits, chains |
| Day 30 | Care Reminder | Cleaning, storage, maintenance |
| Day 60 | Sunglasses Pitch | Prescription sunglasses offer |
| Day 90 | Referral Program | Give £20, Get £20 |

#### Workflow 3: Cart Abandonment

**Trigger:** Cart abandoned (no purchase within 1 hour)
**Duration:** 72 hours

| Timing | Subject Line | Content |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| 1 hour | "Forgot something?" | Cart contents, checkout help |
| 24 hours | "Still thinking it over?" | Social proof, FAQ |
| 72 hours | "Last chance: 5% off" | Small discount to close |

#### Workflow 4: Prescription Expiry

**Trigger:** Prescription uploaded 22 months ago
**Duration:** 60 days

| Timing | Email | Content |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Day 0 | "Time for an eye exam?" | Friendly reminder, eye health importance |
| Day 14 | "Your prescription may be expiring" | Urgency, re-exam guide |
| Day 30 | "New prescription = new glasses" | 15% off with new prescription upload |
| Day 45 | "Don't miss out" | Final reminder |

### 1.4 A/B Testing Strategy

#### Email Elements to Test

| Element | Test Variations | Success Metric |
|---------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Subject Lines** | Emoji vs. no emoji, Question vs. statement, Personalization vs. generic | Open rate |
| **Send Time** | Morning (8am) vs. Evening (6pm) vs. Lunch (12pm) | Open rate |
| **CTA Buttons** | Color (brand vs. contrasting), Text ("Shop Now" vs. "Get My Glasses"), Position | CTR |
| **Personalization** | First name in subject vs. body vs. both | Engagement rate |
| **Email Length** | Short (1 product) vs. Long (6 products) | CTR, conversion |
| **Social Proof** | Star rating vs. Review quote vs. UGC photo | CTR |
| **Discount Framing** | % off vs. £ off vs. Free shipping | Conversion rate |

#### Testing Calendar (Quarterly)

| Week | Test Focus | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|------------|
| 1-2 | Subject lines | Emoji increases open rates for promotional emails |
| 3-4 | Send time | Evening sends perform better for working professionals |
| 5-6 | CTA design | High-contrast buttons outperform brand-color buttons |
| 7-8 | Personalization | Dynamic product recommendations increase CTR |
| 9-10 | Content length | Shorter emails perform better on mobile |
| 11-12 | Incentives | Free shipping outperforms percentage discounts |

### 1.5 Email Best Practices for Eyewear Retail

#### Industry-Specific Tactics

1. **Virtual Try-On Integration**
   - Embed virtual try-on CTAs in product emails
   - Send "See how these look on you" triggered emails
   - Showcase before/after try-on results

2. **Prescription Education**
   - "Understanding your prescription" guide series
   - PD measurement tutorial emails
   - Lens options explained (thinner, coatings, etc.)

3. **Seasonal Campaigns**
   - Back-to-school (August/September)
   - UV awareness (May-July)
   - New Year, new look (January)
   - Blue light awareness (ongoing)

4. **Loyalty & Retention**
   - "Anniversary of your first purchase" emails
   - Early access to new collections
   - Referral program integration

#### Email Deliverability Best Practices

- **Authentication:** Set up SPF, DKIM, DMARC
- **List Hygiene:** Remove inactive subscribers (no opens 6+ months)
- **Segmentation:** Send relevant content to engaged segments
- **Frequency:** Start with 1-2 emails/week, adjust based on engagement
- **Mobile Optimization:** 60%+ opens on mobile; ensure responsive design

### 2.3 Shopping Feed Optimization

#### Feed Structure Best Practices

**Required Attributes (Priority)**

| Attribute | Optimization Strategy |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **Title** | Brand + Model + Frame Type + Key Feature + Gender. Example: "Ray-Ban RB2132 New Wayfarer Tortoise Prescription Glasses - Men Women" |
| **Description** | 150-300 chars, include: style, material, fit, prescription options, UV protection, warranty |
| **Product Type** | Granular taxonomy: Eyewear > Prescription Glasses > Designer > Ray-Ban > Wayfarer |
| **Google Product Category** | 211 (Eyewear) or 2271 (Prescription Eyewear) |
| **Image** | White background, 800x800 min, multiple angles, on-model shots |
| **Price** | Include VAT, competitive pricing, sale price when applicable |
| **Availability** | Accurate stock status, update frequency min 24hrs |
| **Brand** | Exact brand name matching search demand |
| **GTIN/MPN** | Required for designer brands, improves ad serving |

#### Feed Optimization Tactics

**1. Title Optimization Formula**
```
[Brand] + [Model Number] + [Style/Shape] + [Material] + [Key Feature] + [Gender]

Examples:
- "Ray-Ban RB2132 New Wayfarer Tortoise Acetate Prescription Glasses Men Women"
- "Oakley OX8041 Airdrop Titanium Semi-Rimless Sports Glasses Anti-Slip"
- "Affordable Rimless Titanium Ultra-Lightweight Prescription Glasses UK"
```

**2. Custom Labels for Campaign Segmentation**

| Custom Label | Criteria | Use Case |
|--------------|----------|----------|
| **Label 0: Margin** | High (>£50), Medium (£20-50), Low (<£20) | Bid adjustment by profitability |
| **Label 1: Performance** | Best Seller, Standard, Poor Performer | Budget allocation |
| **Label 2: Seasonality** | Year-round, Summer, Winter, BTS | Seasonal bidding |
| **Label 3: Price Range** | Budget (<£50), Mid (£50-150), Premium (£150+) | Audience targeting |
| **Label 4: Frame Type** | Designer, Own Brand, Rimless, etc. | Campaign structure |

**3. Feed Rules & Automation**

```
IF brand IN [Ray-Ban, Oakley, Gucci, Prada] THEN custom_label_0 = "Designer"
IF price < 50 THEN custom_label_3 = "Budget"
IF price > 150 THEN custom_label_3 = "Premium"
IF title CONTAINS "rimless" THEN custom_label_4 = "Rimless"
IF stock_quantity < 5 THEN availability = "limited"
```
